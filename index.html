About

A Life of Purpose and Service

Dr. Ntsiki Tshayingca-Mashiya, PhD

Dr. Nontsikelelo "Ntsiki" Tshayingca-Mashiya is a distinguished South African leader whose career spans the highest levels of government, business, and civil society. Holding a PhD in Management and Commerce from Fort Hare University, she brings deep academic rigour to every endeavour, grounded in the belief that scholarship and service are inseparable.

From the corridors of the Presidency to the boardrooms of the energy sector, from the villages of the Eastern Cape to international stages, Dr. Ntsiki has dedicated her life to one mission: building a South Africa where every voice matters and every community thrives.

Appointed by President Cyril Ramaphosa to lead the transformation programme for South Africa's public sector and State-Owned Entities, she serves as Deputy Director-General in the Department of Planning, Monitoring and Evaluation (DPME) within the Presidency. Her work shapes the institutional backbone of democratic South Africa.

Beyond government, Dr. Ntsiki is a businesswoman, an advocate, a mentor, and a faith-rooted leader. As President of Hightowers Petroleum Africa and CEO of Next Generation Fuel South Africa, she brings the same strategic vision to the private sector that she applies in public service. Through the South Africa Institute for Socio Economic Change (SAISEC) and Wisdom for Women, she coaches and mentors women entrepreneurs and leaders, with particular focus on those in rural communities.

Organisation

Wisdom 4 Women

Wisdom 4 Women is Dr. Ntsiki's passion project: a platform dedicated to empowering women through holistic leadership development, mentoring, and community. Rooted in faith and driven by the conviction that every woman carries wisdom worth sharing, W4W brings together women across South Africa for coaching, collaboration, and transformation.

From established executives to emerging entrepreneurs in rural communities, Wisdom 4 Women creates spaces where women uplift, advise, and propel one another forward.
